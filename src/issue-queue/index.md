Issues are open and manged on Github with the time estimated in the title using the ... markup.

The following standard tags are used to mark issues:

1. Merged
1. Dev
1. Test
1. Live
1. Bug
1. Feature
1. Enhancment
1. Ready for review
1. Released for customer review
1. Markup
1. Invalid
1. Duplicated
1. Question
1. Needs clarification 
1. Missing PSD
1. CSS
1. Priority
1. Can't reproduce
